# ATS Keyword Guidance

Use this reference after generating `fit_analysis.md`.

## Keyword Matching Principles

- Match keywords only when the resume contains the underlying substance.
- Prefer exact JD phrases for common capabilities: "enterprise architecture", "solution architecture", "cloud-native", "GenAI", "agentic AI", "RAG", "Kubernetes", "microservices", "stakeholder management", "delivery assurance".
- Include both acronym and expanded form when useful and truthful, for example "RAG (retrieval-augmented generation)".
- Use standard section headings: Summary, Experience, Skills, Education.
- Avoid keyword stuffing. Keywords should appear naturally in summary, skills, and relevant experience bullets.

## Recruiter Screening Signals

For senior technology leadership roles, make these signals easy to scan when supported:

- Current/recent role scope and level.
- Enterprise architecture or solution architecture ownership.
- AI, GenAI, agentic AI, RAG, LLM orchestration, AI governance, observability, and production deployment.
- Cloud platforms and distributed systems.
- Cross-functional leadership and executive stakeholder influence.
- Delivery scale: budgets, teams, programs, customers, geographies, regulated environments.
- Customer-facing consulting, pre-sales, field engineering, or advisory experience when relevant.

## Output Quality Checks

- The first half page should make the target role fit obvious.
- The Skills section should contain JD keywords that are truthfully supported.
- Each top experience entry should include role-relevant bullets near the top.
- Avoid dense paragraphs; prefer sharp bullets with action, scope, technology, and outcome.
- Keep formatting simple enough for ATS parsing and human scanning.
